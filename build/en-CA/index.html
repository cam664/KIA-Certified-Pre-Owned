NEWS

A message from Ted Lancaster, VP & COO of Kia Canada Inc.

On behalf of Kia Canada, I would like first to thank you for your continued support of the Kia Canada Certified Pre-Owned Program.

As 2017 marks a record year for available pre-owned Kia inventory, I am extremely excited about the opportunities this presents for you, the Kia Brand and the CPO program.

Appropriately, beginning this April KIA will be launching a new and redesigned CPO Monthly Newsletter to capitalize on this occasion. This newsletter will be your source for monthly CPO sales analytics, market trends/performance, industry updates and usage of the current program benefits. It will also include additional tools that will help you drive more sales and profitability when selling Kia CPO vehicles.

Stephane Lubin, National Remarketing & CPO Manager will lead this initiative and will be your immediate resource for further insights and inquiries.

Thank you again for all your efforts and I wish you even greater success moving forward.

Sincerely,

Ted

KIA Canada Car Proof CPO Badging

After mileage and accident history, top current condition is the most important criterion for consumers choosing a used vehicle.

In response, Kia Canada Inc. and CarProof have partnered together to launch the Kia CPO brand logo on Certified Pre-Owned inventory. These Badges will appear on Vehicle History Reports listed on Kia.ca/certified as well as in Kia dealer digital showrooms to identify TRUE Kia CPO Vehicles.

Look for an announcement on the official launch date during the second quarter of this year.
